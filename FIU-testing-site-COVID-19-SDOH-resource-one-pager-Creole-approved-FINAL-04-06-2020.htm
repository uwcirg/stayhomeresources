COVID-19 RESOUS GID KOMINOTẺ

 

Msi pou itilize Florida International University kondwi COVID-19 sit ts la. Gid sa a bay enfmasyon sou asistans resous Miami-Dade si sa neses. Gid sa a ft  pou rezon enfmasyon slman. Resous ki liste anba a, ka chanje souvan. Se poutt sa, nou sijere ke si ou gen nenpt ki kesyon, tanpri rele 3-1-1.

 

  1. Si ou vle enformation piblik sou COVID-19 ki soti nan Depatman Sante Florida a?

Rele Liy Dirk Depatman Sante Florida COVID-19 ki louvri 24 sou 24, sou nimerwo

866-779-6121.

 

  1. Bezwen (resous) ed pou manje?

Sit manje South Florida a, https://feedingsouthflorida.org/covid19/, gen plizy kote nan zn lan ki ap bay manje pou moun ki nan bezwen. Ou kapab ba yo yon apl tou sou 954-518-1818. 

 

  1. Bezwen d ak timoun ki gen laj pou ale lekl?

Lekl Leta Miami - Dade County ofri yon sit entnt ki gen resous pou ede fanmi ak timoun kap aprann lakay yo. http://covid19.dadeschools.net/#!/fullWidth/3024.

 

  1. Bezwen asistans pou resevwa aks entnt a ba-pri oswa gratis?

Komisyon Federal Kominikasyon an bay pwogram ki baze sou revni w pou ede moun ak fanmi yo jwenn aks entnt. Gade sou sit entn yo a, https://www.lifelinesupport.org/, oswa rele Sant Sip Lifeline sou nimerwo 800-234-9473.

 

  1. Si ou ap chche yon nouvo djb oswa asistans pou pale ak patwon ou?

Tcheke sit entnt Career Source South Florida a, www.careersourcesfl.com, oswa rele

305-594-7615 ext. 1526.

 

  1. Bezwen d pou aplike pou benefis konpansasyon pou moun ki pap travay ?

Depatman Optinite Ekonomik nan Florida (The Florida Department of Economic Opportunity) gen yon sit entnt, ki gen lyen pou aplike nan: http://www.floridajobs.org/Reemployment-Assistance-Service-Center/reemployment-assistance/claimants/apply-for-benefits.

 

  1. L ou santi w estrese, dekouraje, oswa enkyete epi ap chche d?

Florida Blue ap bay yon nimerwo telefon gratis pou sip emosyonl pou tout moun ki ap viv nan Florida yo, klkeswa estati asirans yo, sou 833-848-1762, avk konseye disponib nan lang angle ak kreyol.

 

  1. Siw gen kesyon sou svis gouvnman nan konte a oswa sou lt kominote ki san pwofi?

Pou Miami-Dade Couny, rele 3-1-1 oswa 305-468-5900 pou d nan lang Angle, Panyl, oswa Kreyl. Ou ka rele United Way 24 sou 24 nan 2-1-1 oswa 800-226-3320.
